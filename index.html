    About Us

    The Young Muslims Association(TYMA) is a faith based NGO established to tackle pressing challanges faced by muslim children and lay a solid foundationfor their future. With a focus on nuturing islamic values, fostering moral integrity and promoting holistic development. TYMA empowers children with the knowledge, skilss and leadership qualities needed to thrive in a rapidly changing world. Through impactful programs and initiatives, we aim to build a resilent, faith-driven community committed to shaping a brighter future for the Ummah.
